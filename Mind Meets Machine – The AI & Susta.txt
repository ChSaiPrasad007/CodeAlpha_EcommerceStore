Mind Meets Machine – The AI & Sustainability Expert Series



Description
The program features a series of interactive sessions led by IBM experts and 1M1B mentors, providing real-world exposure to advanced AI tools, frameworks, and applications.
Key sessions include:

Understanding SDGs & Foundations of AI/LLMs
Ethics & Responsible AI
Hands-on with IBM Granite Models
Agentic AI – Building Autonomous Systems
Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) Deep Dive
Design Thinking & Project Ideation
Project Mentorship and Prototype Review
Learning Outcomes

By the end of the internship, participants will:

Understand the foundations of AI, ML, and LLMs, and their role in sustainable development.
Apply principles of Responsible AI — ethics, fairness, and transparency.
Gain hands-on experience with IBM Granite Models, RAG, and Agentic AI systems.
Develop and present a capstone project demonstrating AI-powered problem-solving for SDGs.
The link for the sessions are shared with you in the welcome mail.
